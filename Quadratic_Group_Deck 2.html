The Monetization Engine — Why Users Stay and Pay

Agon doesn't monetize like a MOOC. It monetizes like FIFA Ultimate Team — a dual-currency economy with collectible cards, a live marketplace, and a seasonal Battle Pass. Every engagement mechanic is grounded in behavioral economics research and proven at scale in gaming.

Q-Coins
Earned Currency

Earned by studying, completing courses, focus sessions, market replays, leveling up. Variable-ratio reinforcement (random 2×/4× multipliers) creates the same dopamine loop as slot machines — except users are learning finance.

€ → Q
Euro Top-Up

FIFA-style euro-to-Q-Coin exchange. Loss aversion means users value earned Q-Coins more than purchased ones — but the option to buy accelerates engagement when patience runs out.

5% Fee
Marketplace Cut

CS:GO-style continuous double-auction order book. Every card trade generates a 5% house fee. The more users trade, the more revenue flows — zero marginal cost.
